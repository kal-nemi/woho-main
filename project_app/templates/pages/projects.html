Projects

To use / administer projects, a company must be Competence Profiled and Rated.

This means they have undergone the Competency Profiling evaluation by UKCCM staff.

This enables the company to enter the elite Competency Profiling Database, Tender and Partnership functionality on UKCCM and participate as a Profiled Member.

A profiled company can be presented as a Suggested Partner in a tender response, accessing potential New Business, as well as being able to create and administer its own project / cluster spaces etc.

This is place where you can view and edit your project spaces. Using projects, you can communicate and collaborate with your colleagues in a secure manner.
